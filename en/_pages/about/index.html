SUMMARY

For the past 6+ years I've worked with small businesses and start-ups, focusing on scaling organizations through business process optimization and database design & management. I love using my analytical skills to improve the flow of data between different teams and help organizations work more cohesively.


I've worked as the primary architect and administrator for a 150+ user Salesforce environment, including Visualforce and Apex development. I'm also a certified Dell Boomi Professional Developer with experience building custom integrations between CRM, financial and construction management platforms.


I care about living my life with minimal negative impact to the environment and prioritize working with mission based organizations committed to sustainability.
